The calendar reads January 1, 2026. A new cycle begins, and with it, a renewed opportunity to define who we are and what we build.

Last year was a forge. There was fire, there were blows, and there was transformation. Now, the metal is tempered and ready to be sharpened. This year isn't just about surviving the market or learning by force; it's about **executing with mastery**.

## Back to Essence

In a world increasingly saturated with abstractions and automation, my wish for all of us this year is that we don't lose connection with the fundamentals. Let smart tools be our amplifiers, not our substitutes.

I invite every engineer and developer reading this to seek elegance in simplicity. Not to settle for code that "works", but to deeply understand *why* it works. Let this be the year where technical curiosity beats the comfort of the "black box".

## Building with Purpose

May 2026 be the year we leave doubts behind and start building what challenges us. Let every project, big or small, have a clear intention.

Technology is, at the end of the day, a human tool. Let's use it to create real value, to solve complex problems, and to leave the ecosystem a little better than we found it. Let's not seek only speed, but solidity and impact.

## A New Beginning

Thank you for being part of this journey. Whether you are here for the first time or have followed my trajectory, I hope we find in this new cycle a space to share, debate, and grow together.

2026 is a blank canvas (or rather, an empty `main` branch waiting for the first commit). Let's fill it with quality code and stories worth telling.

Happy New Year!

**— Edd.**
